PassPointX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 10, 2025

1. Introduction

PassPointX (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is a secure password manager designed to help you safely store and manage your passwords. Your privacy and security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

2. Data Collection

  • PassPointX does not collect or upload your passwords or any sensitive information.
  • All your credentials are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not access, share, or transmit your data to any third party.
  • PassPointX does not require any account creation, sign-up, or login.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All passwords are encrypted using AES encryption technology.
  • Your data remains on your device and is accessible only via the App.
  • No cloud or server storage is used for your credentials.

4. Offline Usage

PassPointX works entirely offline, ensuring your data is never transmitted over the internet. This makes it fully secure and independent from online networks.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use certain third-party libraries (such as React Native Vector Icons or UI components) to enhance your user experience. However, these libraries do not collect or access your stored passwords. No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are implemented in the App.

6. User Responsibility

  • You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.
  • Do not share your device or unlock credentials with unauthorized users.
  • If your device is lost or damaged, your locally stored data may be lost permanently.

7. Support and Contact

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly. Please review this page periodically to stay informed about how your information is protected.
