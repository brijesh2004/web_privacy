PassPointX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09 October 2025

1. Introduction

PassPointX (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is a secure password manager designed to help you safely store and manage your passwords. Your privacy and security are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

2. Data Collection

  • PassPointX does not collect or upload your passwords or any sensitive information.
  • All your credentials are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not access, share, or transmit your data to any third party.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All passwords are encrypted using AES encryption.
  • Data remains on your device and is accessible only via the App.
  • No cloud or server storage is used for your credentials.

4. Offline Usage

PassPointX works entirely offline, ensuring your data is never transmitted over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use certain libraries (like vector icons or UI components) but these do not access your passwords. No analytics or tracking is implemented.

6. User Responsibility

  • You are responsible for keeping your device secure.
  • Do not share your device with unauthorized users to protect your credentials.
